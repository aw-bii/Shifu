Features

A few things it does well.

All your AI tools in one window
Claude, Gemini, and Opencode all live in one window. Switch between them whenever. The interface stays the same and your history stays put.
Your conversations stay on your machine
Your conversations live on your hard drive, not a cloud server. They save automatically. Search back through everything you've ever asked.
Tell it how to behave, once
Write a short note about how you want the AI to act: the tone, the focus, the format. Save it, pick it when you open a new conversation.
Works offline, ships local
Everything runs on your machine. No data leaves your computer. Your workflow stays private and fast, even without internet.
