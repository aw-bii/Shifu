# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Analysts and researchers at Bertelsmann India Investment who use the tool occasionally — running research queries, synthesizing information, or comparing AI backend outputs. They are not engineers, and the tool is not a daily driver. They come to it with a specific task in mind and expect it to stay out of their way while they complete it.

## Product Purpose

A unified desktop interface for interacting with multiple AI CLI backends (Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Opencode). Eliminates the need to switch between terminals or tools — one place to run queries, manage personas, and read structured responses. Success means: open it, ask something, get a useful answer, close it.

## Brand Personality

Precise, Focused, Minimal. The tool should feel like a cleared desk — no clutter, no ceremony, just the work. Calm rather than clinical. Composed rather than flashy.

## Anti-references

- ChatGPT / Claude.ai web — consumer-facing gradients, marketing gloss, "AI product" visual language
- Notion AI or Copilot-in-Word — feature-packed sidebars, templates, heavy onboarding
- Generic SaaS dashboards — hero metrics, card grids, colourful sidebar icons
- Anything with gradient text, glassmorphism, or animated pulse loaders as a primary affordance

## Design Principles

1. **Serve the query, not the interface.** Every screen element that doesn't directly support reading or writing a message is a distraction. Reduce ruthlessly.
2. **Calm competence.** Whitespace is not emptiness — it's the visual equivalent of a quiet room. Use it to signal that the tool is in control, not overwhelmed.
3. **Hierarchy through space, not decoration.** Differentiate content levels with size, weight, and margin — not color blocks, borders, or card containers.
4. **Occasional use, low friction.** Because users open this tool intermittently, the interface must be instantly readable without a learning curve. No hidden affordances, no unlabelled icons.
5. **Respect the content.** AI-generated text is the product. Typography and layout should make long markdown responses pleasant to read — not compete with them.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Full keyboard navigability for all actions. Dark mode supported throughout (primary working mode for many users). No reliance on color alone to convey state.
